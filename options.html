Hiring Cafe Watcher

Configure the keyword to monitor and the email delivery webhook.

Used for https://hiring.cafe/api/search-jobs?search=<keyword> requests.
This endpoint should accept a JSON body of {"to", "subject", "body"} and deliver an email. Services like SendGrid, Mailgun, Postmark, EmailJS, or a Zapier/Make webhook can be used here.
Minimum interval is 15 minutes. Defaults to 120 minutes (2 hours).
